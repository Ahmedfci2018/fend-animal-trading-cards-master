Giant Panda

A giant panda's paw has six digits - five fingers and an opposable pseudo-thumb it uses merely to hold bamboo while eating.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Length: 4 to 6 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Cool, wet bamboo forests

The life span of giant pandas in the wild is approximately 20 years. Captive pandas may live up to 25-30 years. The giant panda is a conservation reliant endangered species, and their wild population estimates vary anywhere from around 1600 to 3000.
